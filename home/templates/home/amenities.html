Here To Serve You

The Harborage at Ashley Marina is located on the downtown Charleston, SC peninsula within walking distance to nearby historical sites, restaurants, and shops. It is the perfect location to dock your vessel whether temporarily or long-term. The Harborage at Ashley Marina accommodates vessels up to 120 feet in length with approximately 230 slips conveniently located north of the James Island Expressway on the Ashley River offering shelter from strong winds and seas. So, whether an investor, owner, transient, boat or yacht enthusiast you will be made to feel at home here at The Harborage at Ashley Marina!
