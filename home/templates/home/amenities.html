Marina Amenities


Here To Serve You

The Harborage at Ashley Marina is located on the downtown Charleston, SC peninsula within walking distance to nearby historical sites, restaurants, and shops. It is the perfect location to dock your vessel whether temporarily or long-term. The Harborage at Ashley Marina accommodates vessels up to 120 feet in length with approximately 230 slips conveniently located north of the James Island Expressway on the Ashley River offering shelter from strong winds and seas. So, whether an investor, owner, transient, boat or yacht enthusiast you will be made to feel at home here at The Harborage at Ashley Marina!


Gated Access

Secure entry to the marina with private gated access.

Bathroom Facilities

The Harborage at Ashley Marina provides 24hr access to bathrooms for ladies and gentlemen with restrooms and large showers. An additional restroom can be found in the laundry facility on the first floor.

Laundry Facilities

Ashley Marina has two coin operated washers and dryers available for owner, tenant, and transient use with 24hr access. Change can be provided at the T dock office from 8-6pm.

Free Wifi

Every slip can access fast, reliable wifi at no additional cost, thanks to On-Spot Wifi and their access points installed throughout the Harborage at Ashley Marina. *Please note that onSpot restricts devices that repeat the wifi signal.

Utilities

Water and shore-power are provided at each slip. Costs are included in the quarterly regime fee. Transients are assessed a cord fee. A single 30-amp and 50-amp hook-up are available at most slips. A limited number of 100-amp single phase and 100-amp 3-phase 208v outlets are available on T-dock.

Parking

Each slip is granted one parking permit. There are a limited number of guest parking spaces limited to 24hrs. Parking is first come first serve. Off-site paid parking is available at the WestEdge Publix garage and Safe Harbour Charleston City Marina.

Marine Fuel

We offer two high speed off-road diesel pumps, with flow rates up to 40 gpm, available on a portion of T-dock. Two non-ethanol 89-octane Marine Gasoline pumps are located on C-dock at the end of the fixed walkway. Fuel pumps shut down 30-minutes before office hours.

Fish Cleaning Station

A fish cleaning station is available on C dock.

Free Pump Out Station

Thanks to DNR's clean water initiatives, the Harborage at Ashley Marina is proud to provide free pump-outs of waste tanks. Waste can be pumped 8am-5:30pm on the T-dock. A mobile pump-out boat is available on Thursday morning. Scheduling for this is done at Maintenance@myashleymarina.com

Dock Carts

Dock carts are available for slip-owners, tenants, and transients to use for those big provisioning runs or hauling maintenance items to slips from the parking area.
