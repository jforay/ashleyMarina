Marriott Rates


Current owners, slip renters, and transient guests staying at the Harborage at Ashley Marina may enjoy a discounted rate at the Charleston Courtyard Marriott. After you successfully book with us, you will receive a confirmation email that contains a link to book a room at the Marriott for 25% off!
